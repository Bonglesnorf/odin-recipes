Macaroni

Description

A three ingredient macaroni and cheese recipe (not including salt and water) developed by J. Kenji LÃ³pez-Alt

There are a few tricks that make this recipe work such as the minimal use of water which allows the starch from the pasta to be far more concentrated which makes for a creamier sauce

The other trick is the use of evaporated milk which has powerful emulsifying agents which also contribute to a creamy sauce

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces (170g) elbow macaroni
  • Salt
  • 6 ounces (180ml) evaporated milk
  • 6 ounces (170g) grated mild or medium cheddar cheese
